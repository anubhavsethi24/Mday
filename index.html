Things I Need You to Know 💌

🌅
The first moment I knew

There's a specific memory — maybe ordinary to everyone else — where I looked at you and just knew. That this person is different. That whatever this is, it matters. I think about that moment more than you'd ever guess.

🤍
The little things you do

The way you check on me without making it a big deal. The way you remember things I said in passing. The warmth in your voice even on hard days. You don't even realise how extraordinary your ordinary is.

🌧️
When things were hard

There were days when everything felt like too much. You didn't give up on me, you fixed it. That effort? That's the kind of love people write songs about. That's the kind I want to keep forever.

🏠
You feel like home

Home isn't always a place. Sometimes it's the comfort of someone's voice, the safety of their energy, the way they make you feel like you can exhale. You are that for me. Completely and effortlessly.

💪
Your strength amazes me

You carry so much — for the ones you love, for the world around you — and you do it with a grace that makes me both proud and in awe. Watching you love fiercely is one of the greatest privileges of my life.

🌙
For what's ahead

More adventures. More late nights. More memories we haven't made yet. I can't wait for all of it — every ordinary day, every ridiculous laugh, every quiet moment — as long as it's with you.

💗

Thank You

Thank you for being you — with all your warmth, your little quirks, your ridiculous laugh, and your enormous heart. Thank you for the way you love — not just loudly or gently, but consistently, even on the days when everything is heavy.

You didn't have to show up the way you do. But you do it anyway, every single time, without making it a big deal. That's the thing about you that moves me the most. The love you give isn't a production — it just quietly exists, steady and generous and real.

I built this because you deserve to feel appreciated — not just today, not just on the easy days, but always. Happy Mother's Day to the woman who makes life softer. I love you. A ridiculous, embarrassing, full-hearted amount. 💗
